Hello, I'm Jennifer Darling

I am a highly qualified CBT therapist, supervisor, lecturer and mindfulness teacher based in Manchester.

Mindfulness

I am an experienced and qualified mindfulness practitioner and teacher/trainer. I offer Mindfulness-based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT) courses in a relaxed and friendly group setting.

Supervision

I am a BABCP accredited supervisor and offer supervision for CBT practitioners, supervisors, trainees, health professionals and HR professionals. CBT supervision can be offered in groups or individually.

1-1 therapy

I provide a personalised and confidential cognitive behavioural therapy service for adults aged 18 and over. I have experience in working with clients from many different backgrounds both in the NHS and privately.

Teaching

I teach and help run the DIP he CBT course Sheffield University and am a BABCP accredited trainer / teacher. I have a wealth of teaching experience at pre and post qualification level and have also taught at other institutions such as Leeds Met and Leeds University.

About me

I have been working in the mental health field since 1996, and am an accredited CBT practitioner, supervisor and trainer.

During my mental health career I have worked in a variety of settings including, inpatient and outpatient work, GP Surgeries, the NHS, Charities and the Voluntary and private sector. As an experienced CBP practitioner I have published articles in several journals and undertaken research, facilitated trainings, organised workshops.

Currently I am a clinical lecturer in cognitive behaviour therapy in Sheffield. I am currently undertaking mindfulness teacher training, I have been a mindfulness practitioner for the past 3 years.

I am an advocate of mindfulness based interventions I am experienced in supervision and supervise other CBP therapists and trainees. Having a great understanding from teaching of the skills needed for effective CBT. I am also fully conversant with ethical codes of practice and confidentiality. Working in many areas has provided me with a wealth of knowledge and experience which I utilise to help individuals, couples, professionals, families and organisations create meaningful, long lasting and useful change.
